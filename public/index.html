Surya Namaskar (Sun Salutation)

Duration: 10-15 minutes | Difficulty: Intermediate

Mental Health Benefits:

  • ‚Ä¢ Reduces stress and anxiety
  • ‚Ä¢ Improves mood and energy
  • ‚Ä¢ Enhances focus and concentration
  • ‚Ä¢ Promotes better sleep
  • ‚Ä¢ Increases self-awareness

Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. Prayer Pose (Pranamasana)

Stand straight with feet together. Bring palms together at heart center. Breathe normally and center yourself.

2. Raised Arms (Hastauttanasana)

Inhale and lift arms overhead. Arch back slightly, keeping arms shoulder-width apart. Look up at your hands.

3. Forward Bend (Uttanasana)

Exhale and bend forward from hips. Try to touch the ground beside your feet. Keep legs straight if possible.

4. Low Lunge (Ashwa Sanchalanasana)

Inhale and step right leg back into lunge. Keep left knee over ankle. Look up and open chest.

5. Plank Pose (Dandasana)

Hold breath and step left leg back. Form straight line from head to heels. Engage core muscles.

6. Eight-Point Pose (Ashtanga Namaskara)

Exhale and lower knees, chest, and chin to ground. Keep hips slightly raised. Eight points touch the floor.

7. Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana)

Inhale and slide forward, lifting chest. Keep pelvis on ground. Arch back gently, looking up.

8. Downward Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)

Exhale and lift hips up, forming inverted V. Straighten arms and legs. Press palms firmly down.

9-12. Return Sequence

Repeat steps 4, 3, 2, 1 in reverse order to complete one full round of Surya Namaskar.

Vrikshasana (Tree Pose)

Duration: 30 seconds - 1 minute each side | Difficulty: Beginner

Mental Health Benefits:

  • ‚Ä¢ Improves balance and focus
  • ‚Ä¢ Reduces anxiety and stress
  • ‚Ä¢ Builds confidence and stability
  • ‚Ä¢ Enhances concentration
  • ‚Ä¢ Promotes mindfulness

Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. Mountain Pose

Stand straight with feet together, arms at sides. Distribute weight evenly on both feet. Engage core muscles.

2. Shift Weight

Slowly shift your weight to your left foot. Feel grounded and stable on your standing leg.

3. Lift Right Foot

Bend right knee and place right foot on left inner thigh, above or below knee (never on knee).

4. Prayer Position

Bring palms together at heart center. Press foot into leg and leg into foot for stability.

5. Find Focus Point

Fix your gaze on a point in front of you. This helps maintain balance and concentration.

6. Hold & Breathe

Hold for 30 seconds to 1 minute. Breathe deeply and steadily. Repeat on the other side.

Balasana (Child's Pose)

Duration: 1-3 minutes

Steps:

  1. Kneel on the floor with big toes touching
  2. Sit back on your heels
  3. Separate knees about hip-width apart
  4. Exhale and lay torso down between thighs
  5. Extend arms forward or alongside body
  6. Rest forehead on the ground
  7. Breathe deeply and relax

Mental Health Benefits:

Calms the mind, relieves stress and anxiety, promotes introspection and peace

Anulom Vilom (Alternate Nostril Breathing)

Duration: 5-10 minutes

Steps:

  1. Sit comfortably with spine straight
  2. Use right thumb to close right nostril
  3. Inhale slowly through left nostril
  4. Close left nostril with ring finger
  5. Release thumb and exhale through right nostril
  6. Inhale through right nostril
  7. Close right nostril, release left
  8. Exhale through left nostril
  9. This completes one cycle

Mental Health Benefits:

Balances nervous system, reduces stress, improves focus, calms anxiety

Mindfulness Meditation Video

Mindfulness Meditation

Duration: 10-20 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Find a comfortable seated position
  2. Close your eyes or soften your gaze
  3. Focus on your natural breath
  4. Notice when your mind wanders
  5. Gently return attention to breath
  6. Observe thoughts without judgment
  7. Continue for desired duration

Benefits:

Reduces anxiety, improves focus, increases self-awareness, promotes emotional regulation

ü´ß

Body Scan Meditation Video

Body Scan Meditation

Duration: 15-30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Lie down comfortably on your back
  2. Close your eyes and breathe naturally
  3. Start by focusing on your toes
  4. Slowly move attention up through your body
  5. Notice sensations in each body part
  6. Don't try to change anything, just observe
  7. End at the top of your head

Benefits:

Reduces physical tension, improves body awareness, promotes relaxation, helps with sleep

üíù

Loving-Kindness Meditation Video

Loving-Kindness Meditation

Duration: 10-15 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Sit comfortably and close your eyes
  2. Begin by sending love to yourself
  3. Repeat: "May I be happy, may I be healthy"
  4. Extend love to a loved one
  5. Send love to a neutral person
  6. Include someone you have difficulty with
  7. Finally, send love to all beings

Benefits:

Increases compassion, reduces negative emotions, improves relationships, boosts positive feelings

üåÖ

Guided Visualization Video

Guided Visualization

Duration: 10-20 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Find a quiet, comfortable space
  2. Close your eyes and relax
  3. Imagine a peaceful, safe place
  4. Engage all your senses in the visualization
  5. Notice colors, sounds, smells, textures
  6. Stay in this peaceful place
  7. Slowly return to the present when ready

Benefits:

Reduces stress, enhances creativity, improves mood, promotes deep relaxation
